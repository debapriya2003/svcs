Admission

Admission is granted in Class I to the children who have completed five (5) years by 1st March of the calendar year. Admission in other classes will be against vacancies. If the school Authority thinks it necessary, the required documents for admission are:
➤ The mark sheet of the last Annual/Half yearly examination.
➤ Attested copy of Govt. Approved Birth Certificate.
➤ Transfer certificate in Original
➤ Three (3) copies of photographs of the child.
