Co-curricular Activities

The Spring Valley Central School does not focus solely on academic skills but provides a holistic program of co-curricular activities such as, sports, yoga, drama, physical training etc. The students are trained by professional coaches from a young age. A variety of age-related recreational and competitive sports are offered both during and outside Physical Education classes. Students are encouraged to develop their talents and demonstrate them during the many school and festival celebrations (Ex. Republic day, independence day, teachers day, children day etc) organized in the course of the academic year. The Annual Sports Meet held mid-year and the Annual School Day marking the end of the school year are occasions that highlight both academic and co-curricular excellence.

To raise competitiveness among students, the students of the school are distributed in four houses. Every year during Annual Sports Meet all the houses participates in various Activities/Games with full enthusiasm. Prizes and School based certificate of appreciation are issued to winners.

Health Club

The School has constituted a health club to look after well being of its Children. Every year it organizes health camp with Expert doctors, which helps in identifying various health problems among childrens at primitive level.

Eco Club

The school constituted an Eco Club running under guidance of Vigyan Mandir, Hailakandi. The main objective is to create awareness among children about preservation of nature. The school celebrates world environment day every year by organizing awareness programmes and plantations. To maintain health & hygiene in campus Students regularly participates in "Swachchata Abhiyan" conducted by School.
