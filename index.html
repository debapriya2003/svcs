Spring Valley Central School, Hailakandi established in the year 2011, is a Co-educational English medium Senior Secondary School. The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi. The school is situated in Lala Road Niscintapur, Hailakandi. The Institution is an educational & research initiative of the trust ENR WELFARE FOUNDATION, GUWAHATI.

Principal's Message

Welcome to Spring Valley Central School, where we believe in nurturing young minds and shaping future leaders. Our commitment to excellence in education goes beyond academics to include character building, moral values, and holistic development.

At Spring Valley, we strive to create an environment where every student can discover their potential, explore their talents, and develop the skills necessary for success in the 21st century. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that each child receives personalized attention and quality education.

We invite you to join our school family and be part of this exciting journey of learning and growth.

Ms. Parveen Sultana Barbhuiya
Principal
