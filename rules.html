Rules & Regulations - Spring Valley Central School

Every student enrolled in Spring Valley Central School is expected to maintain discipline and follow the code of conduct both inside and outside the classroom. The following rules and regulations must be observed:

  • 1. Students must be punctual and attend school regularly.
  • 2. Proper school uniform is compulsory on all working days and school functions.
  • 3. Students should maintain discipline and show respect to teachers, staff, and fellow classmates.
  • 4. Any kind of misbehavior, use of foul language, or bullying will not be tolerated.
  • 5. Students must take care of school property and avoid scribbling or damaging furniture, books, or walls.
  • 6. Carrying mobile phones, electronic gadgets, or unauthorized items is strictly prohibited.
  • 7. Students must complete homework and assignments on time.
  • 8. Participation in school activities, functions, and assemblies is mandatory.
  • 9. Students should maintain cleanliness and hygiene in classrooms and school premises.
  • 10. Any leave of absence must be informed in writing by parents/guardians in advance.
  • 11. Students are expected to uphold the reputation of the school by exhibiting good behavior outside the campus as well.

Failure to comply with the above rules may result in disciplinary action by the school authorities.
